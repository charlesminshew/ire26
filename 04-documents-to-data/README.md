# Session 4 — Turning Documents into Data with AI

**Sunday 10:15 AM · 1 hour · Maryland 1-2**

Agencies produce mountains of PDFs, scanned records, and semi-structured documents that are useless until you can query them. This session covers a repeatable workflow for using AI to extract structured data from documents at scale.

## What you'll learn

- How to structure a document-extraction prompt for consistent output
- Getting AI to output JSON or CSV you can actually use
- Handling scanned documents and OCR errors
- Validating AI-extracted data before you publish
- When AI extraction is good enough — and when it isn't

## What to bring

- Laptop (no tablets)
- Free account at [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai)
- A document you'd like to turn into data (optional)
